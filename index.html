Tracking State Anxiety Across the Menstrual Cycle in Naturally Cycling Women

This research aims to explore how general anxiety levels vary throughout the phases of the menstrual cycle in women who are naturally cycling, without using hormonal contraceptives.

Participate Now

Overview

Conducting a longitudinal study tracking state anxiety levels across two menstrual cycles in women who are naturally cycling, without using hormonal contraceptives.

Eligibility

  • Female
  • Age 18+
  • Natural menstrual cycle
  • Not using hormonal birth control
  • No hormonal birth control in last 2 months

Methodology

  • Data collection over two full cycles
  • Daily anxiety surveys
  • Theory of Mind tests during follicular and luteal phases
